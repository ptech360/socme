Features

It is a free to use connectivity tool whereby influencers
and businesses can add value to one-another without excessive agency fees.

  • Intuitive Data

    We have adapted a platform in which businesses are able to search by number of followers, number of posts, targeted demographic, region and impressions made.

  • Connectivity

    Socme is a social platform in which allows influencers and businesses to connect and stay connected with the ability to form business social circles. We allow both influencers and businesses to rate one another based on value added.

  • User Friendly

    Socme provides an unbeatable user experience, while keeping it simple and elegant.

  • Influencers

    Socme is a free to use tool to grow your own business and your social empire. Add value to the advertising world and to businesses in which you represent!

  • Businesses

    This is a catalogue of influencers powered by statistics and social presence, you are able to sort by price, reach and many other essential areas which would best benefit your business

  • Cross Platform

    Influencers are able to show case their work across all platforms. Instagram, Twitter, Facebook and YouTube. This will give businesses the opportunity to find the perfect influencer seamlessly.
